# Assets

You can use 3D models, images, and sounds to make your creations more interesting. Here are some resources for finding and creating assets.

## Finding Assets

There are many websites where you can find free and paid assets:

* **Sketchfab:** A large library of 3D models.
* **Poly Pizza:** A collection of low-poly 3D models.
* **Kenney.nl:** A great source for game assets, including 2D and 3D models.
* **Freesound:** A collaborative database of Creative Commons licensed sounds.

## Creating Assets

If you want to create your own assets, here are some popular tools:

* **Blender:** A free and open-source 3D creation suite.
* **MagicaVoxel:** A free, lightweight 8-bit voxel art editor.
* **Audacity:** A free, open-source, cross-platform audio software.
